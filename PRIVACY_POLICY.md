# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: October 3, 2025**

This privacy policy applies to the FluPass app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Orkun Dayı (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

## Information Collection and Use

**FluPass is a privacy-first password manager that prioritizes your data security and privacy.**

### Data Storage

**All your data is stored locally on your device only.** The Application does NOT:

* Collect or transmit your passwords to any server
* Share your credentials with any third party
* Require account registration or sign-up
* Store any of your personal information on external servers
* Track your usage or behavior

Your sensitive information including:
* Saved passwords and credentials
* Credit card information
* Security settings
* Imported/exported data

**remains exclusively on your device** and is encrypted using industry-standard encryption methods.

### Device Information

The Application does NOT collect any device information, usage statistics, or analytics data. We do not track:

* IP addresses
* Device identifiers
* Usage patterns
* Location data
* App analytics

### Security Features

The Application provides the following security features to protect your data:

* **Local Encryption**: All data is encrypted on your device
* **Biometric Authentication**: Face ID, Touch ID, or fingerprint authentication
* **Master Password**: Optional password protection
* **Autofill Service**: Secure password autofill for apps and websites (data never leaves your device)
* **Import/Export**: Ability to backup and restore your data (encrypted)

## Third Party Access

**The Application does NOT use any third-party services** for analytics, advertising, or data collection. 

The Application does NOT share any data with third parties because:
* No data is collected from users
* No analytics services are integrated
* No advertising networks are used
* No cloud synchronization services are employed

## Permissions

The Application may request the following permissions:

* **Biometric/Fingerprint**: To enable biometric authentication for app security
* **Autofill Service**: To provide password autofill functionality in other apps
* **Storage**: To import/export encrypted backup files

These permissions are used solely for the intended security features and do not result in any data collection or transmission.

## Data Import/Export

The Application allows you to:

* **Import** password data from encrypted backup files
* **Export** your data to encrypted backup files

These files are:
* Created and stored locally on your device
* Encrypted with your master password
* Never transmitted to any external server
* Your sole responsibility to manage and secure

## No Account Required

The Application does NOT require:
* Account registration
* Email address
* Phone number
* Personal information
* Internet connection (works completely offline)

## Data Retention Policy

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

* The Service Provider does NOT retain any of your data
* All data is deleted when you uninstall the Application
* You have complete control over your data at all times

## Children

The Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of children's data being compromised.

## Security

The Service Provider is committed to ensuring the security of your information:

* **End-to-End Encryption**: All data is encrypted locally on your device
* **Zero-Knowledge Architecture**: The Service Provider has no access to your data
* **Biometric Protection**: Industry-standard biometric authentication
* **No Data Transmission**: Your data never leaves your device
* **Open Source Security**: Uses proven encryption libraries

You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.
